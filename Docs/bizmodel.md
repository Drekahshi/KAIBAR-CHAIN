### 2. Detailed Revenue Streams

#### 2.1 Transaction Fees (30% of Revenue = $720K/year)
Every user transaction (send, receive, swap, stake) incurs a small fee. Fee structure is dynamic (0.1% baseline, up to 0.8% during peak liquidity). Allocated: 50% to liquidity providers, 20% to treasury, 30% burned (deflationary).

**Calculation (Year 1)**:  
100,000 users × 10 transactions/user/month × 12 months = 12M transactions/year  
Average transaction: $100 × 0.3% average fee = $0.30 per transaction  
12M transactions × $0.30 = $3.6M in fees  
KAI protocol captures: 20% = **$720K** (80% goes to LPs and burns)

**User Impact**:  
User sends $100 → pays $0.30 fee (75% cheaper than traditional banks).

#### 2.2 Trust Management Fees (25% of Revenue = $600K/year)
One-time creation fee + scaled annual maintenance + distribution fee on inheritance release.

**Fee Structure**:

| Fee Type              | Amount          | Frequency     | Revenue (Y1) |
|-----------------------|-----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Trust Creation        | $10 flat        | Once per trust| $300K        |
| Annual Maintenance    | 0.5% of TVL     | Annually      | $200K        |
| Inheritance Release   | 1% of distribution | One-time   | $100K        |
| **TOTAL**             |                 |               | **$600K**    |

**User Experience**:  
$10 one-time fee to set up lifetime wealth protection (vs $5,000–$15,000 traditional trust).

#### 2.3 Yield Optimization Fees (20% of Revenue = $480K/year)
KAI’s AI rebalances capital daily. Users pay a performance fee only when yield exceeds benchmark.

**Calculation**:  
$100M TVL × 24% blended APY = $24M total yield  
Benchmark 20% APY → excess 4% = $4M  
KAI captures 10% of excess = **$480K**

**User Benefit**:  
Users only pay when KAI outperforms the market.

#### 2.4 Insurance Premiums (15% of Revenue = $360K/year)
Management fee from peer-to-peer pools; remaining capital earns yield to cover claims.

**Pool Economics**:  
10,000 members × $100 average premium = $1M pooled  
KAI fee (30%) = $300K + yield on capital = **$360K** (conservative)

#### 2.5–2.7 Remaining Streams
SocialFi/GAMI licensing, partnership integrations, and staking rewards complete the model at **$240K combined** — all scaling naturally with user growth.
