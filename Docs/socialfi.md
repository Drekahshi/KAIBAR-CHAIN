## 1. Abstract

**Kai SocialFi** is a decentralized protocol that turns every online action — likes, comments, reading, watching, fitness tracking, and creative participation — into **real, private, verifiable rewards**.

Powered by **agentic AI**, **zero-knowledge proofs**, and **Hedera’s deterministic consensus**, Kai creates a user-owned, creator-driven ecosystem where attention generates value that flows directly to users and creators — not Silicon Valley middlemen.

**Core Innovation on Hedera**:  
- Attention verification through deterministic oracles  
- Instant 3–6 second settlement  
- Zero MEV (no front-running)  
- Complete privacy via ZK proofs  
- Immutable proof of every engagement on the ledger  

**The Promise**: A teenager in Nairobi earns $50–$500 per month from watching videos and engaging with content — privately, instantly, and with full ownership of their data and earnings.

## 4. Architecture Overview (Hedera-Native)

Kai SocialFi is built in six clean layers optimized for Hedera:

1. **Client Layer** — Web, mobile, browser extension, and USSD for universal access  
2. **Agentic AI Engine** — Classifies genuine engagement quality (99%+ fraud prevention)  
3. **ZK Proof Engine** — Proves engagement happened without revealing any private data  
4. **Smart Contracts** — Manage rewards, quests, and GAMI distribution with deterministic execution  
5. **Backend Infrastructure** — TheGraph indexing, IPFS storage, offline sync  
6. **Cross-Platform Integration** — YouTube, TikTok, Twitter, Lens, Farcaster + gasless onboarding  

All settlement happens on Hedera (3–6 second finality, near-zero fees, zero MEV).

## 5. How It Works (Step-by-Step)

### Phase 1: Onboarding (60 Seconds)
1. Open app or browser → Sign in with phone/email  
2. OTP verification (1 click)  
3. Wallet auto-created and secured by biometric/passcode  
4. Ready to earn instantly — no seed phrases, no crypto purchase  

### Phase 2: Engagement Tracking
Your device + AI tracks activity → generates ZK proof → sends to Hedera oracle → smart contract verifies and rewards in 3–6 seconds.

### Phase 3: Reward Calculation & Distribution
Example (15-minute video watch):
- Base reward: 2 GAMI  
- Quality (95% attention): +10%  
- Creator multiplier: +50%  
- Streak bonus: +20%  
- **Total: 3.96 GAMI**  
- User receives 60% (2.37 GAMI)  
- Creator receives 30%  
- Protocol receives 10% (operations)  

All atomic and immutable on Hedera.

### Phase 4: Gamified Progression
RPG-style levels, daily quests, weekly challenges, and streak multipliers turn normal scrolling into a rewarding habit.

### Phase 5: Creator Studio
Creators set their own reward values, launch tokenized quests, run sponsorships, and receive analytics — all with full control.
