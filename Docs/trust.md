## 4. Architecture (Hedera-Native Layers)

KAI Trust is built in four clean layers that work together perfectly on Hedera:

**Layer 1: Smart Contract Core**  
The unbreakable rules. Once you create a trust, the terms are locked forever on Hedera’s immutable ledger.

**Layer 2: Agentic AI Layer**  
Your personal, unbiased guardian. The AI watches conditions 24/7, harvests yields daily, rebalances assets, and executes distributions exactly as you wrote them — with zero human bias.

**Layer 3: Trustee Consensus Layer**  
Human oversight only when needed. A 3-of-5 multi-sig DAO of elected trustees handles rare edge cases or disputes (less than 1% of trusts). All decisions are public and final on Hedera.

**Layer 4: Privacy Layer**  
Zero-knowledge proofs ensure sensitive events (death, graduation, medical needs) are verified without ever revealing private details on-chain.

Every action settles in 3–6 seconds. Every record is permanent. Every distribution is fair and automatic.
